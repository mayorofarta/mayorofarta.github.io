Savanimals

Another Android Game.

Savanimals is an educational game for kids. Through smooth progressing and fun mechanics they will be able to learn animals' names (mostly living in Africa's savannas) and recognize them based on their characteristics. The game will also test their observation, insight and reflexes.

Features: 1 Campaign (Safari) mode 3 Extra Modes to unlock Highscores to beat

Available in: English Greek
